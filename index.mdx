Whether you're building a content pipeline, automating video production, or prototyping a new product, TechBlazing gets you from idea to video in minutes.

## Get up and running in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for TechBlazing">
    Create your free account at [techblazing.com/signup](https://techblazing.com/signup). No credit card is required to get started — you receive 10 free generations each month on the free tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    In the dashboard, navigate to **Settings → API Keys** and click **Create Key**. Copy the key and store it somewhere safe — you won't be able to view it again after closing the dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a generation">
    Send a `POST /v1/generations` request with your prompt, style preferences, and desired aspect ratio. TechBlazing returns a generation ID you'll use to track progress.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your video">
    Poll `GET /v1/generations/{id}` until the status changes to `completed`, then fetch the `output_url` to download your finished MP4 video.
  </Step>
</Steps>
